About MoneyCal

Simple calculators for everyday money decisions — built to load fast, work on any phone, and never ask you to sign up.

What we do

MoneyCal is a growing collection of free finance and insurance calculators. Each one does a single job well: estimate a monthly payment, project savings growth, compare debt-payoff strategies. No accounts, no paywalls, no email capture before you get an answer.

How our calculators work

Every calculator runs entirely in your browser. We don't send your numbers to a server or store them anywhere — the math happens on your device and disappears when you close the tab. The formulas behind each tool are standard, documented financial calculations, and every tool page explains the method it uses.

A note on accuracy

Our tools are built for estimation and education. They use well-established formulas, but real-world results depend on details we can't see — your exact interest rate, fees, taxes, and personal circumstances. Always confirm important figures with your lender, a licensed insurance agent, or a qualified financial professional before making a decision.
